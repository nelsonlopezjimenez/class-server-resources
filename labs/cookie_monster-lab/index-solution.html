Victoria's Journal

So fresh and so clean

11/12/08: Best Day Ever

Today was the best day ever! I got to meet Patrick, the most adorable golden retriever puppy. He was so fluffy and playful. We spent the whole afternoon at the dog park, and he even learned to fetch! I can't wait to see him again next week. Life is good when you have furry friends.

Later in the evening, I tried that new cafe downtown. The atmosphere was perfect - cozy lighting, smooth jazz playing in the background, and the best latte I've ever had. I brought my sketchbook and spent two hours just drawing and people-watching. These are the moments I live for.

11/11/08: Sunny Day at the Beach

Woke up early and drove to the coast. The weather was absolutely perfect - not a cloud in the sky! I walked along the shore for hours, collecting seashells and watching the waves crash against the rocks. Found a beautiful piece of sea glass that I'm going to turn into a pendant.

The sunset was breathtaking. I sat on the beach with my camera and captured some amazing photos. The sky turned brilliant shades of orange, pink, and purple. Sometimes I forget how therapeutic nature can be. Definitely need to make these beach trips a regular thing.

11/10/08: Lazy Sunday

Sometimes the best days are the ones where you do absolutely nothing productive. Spent the entire day in my pajamas, reading that mystery novel I've been putting off. Made myself hot chocolate with extra marshmallows and just relaxed.

In the afternoon, I FaceTimed with my college roommate. We laughed about old memories and made plans to meet up next month. It's crazy how time flies, but true friendships never fade. Feeling grateful for the simple things today.

11/09/08: Cooking Adventures

Decided to challenge myself and make pasta from scratch. It was messier than I expected! Flour everywhere, dough sticking to my hands, but the end result was totally worth it. There's something satisfying about creating food with your own hands.

Had some friends over for dinner and they were all impressed. We had such a great time - good food, good wine, and lots of laughter. These are the memories I want to hold onto. Already planning next week's menu!
